Adamstown

Adamstown is the capital and only town of Pitcairn Island. This is where all the facilities are located and Pitkerners gather.

Adamstown Main square
The main square of the city where all the Pitcairners gather.
The Post Office
located near the main square
The church
The 7th day adventist church next to the main square
The island's school Pulau
The school lies up in the western "suburbs" of Adamstown.
Christian's Cafe
The only bar and restaurant on Pitcairn, opens only on friday after 6:30pm.
The Bounty's anchor
On display in front of the Public Hall in the town square, where the library/post office building, and the Adventist church can also be found.
The general store
The general store is open 2 mornings a week
The Museum
contains artifacts from the Bounty, stamps, issues of National Geographic featuring the islands, and other items of local interest. One of the ship's four cannons is displayed there.
The health center
The health center of Pitcairn, well equipped for basic injuries
